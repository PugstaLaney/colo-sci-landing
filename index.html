What makes Colo different

Built for how science actually works

Most AI tools summarize the literature. Colo stress-tests it. The difference is a verdict you can defend.

Adversarial reasoning

Two agents with opposing research orientations challenge every claim before consensus is reached. Conclusions survive scrutiny because they had to earn it.

Citation provenance verification

Every PMID cited by an agent is checked against the corpus in real time. Hallucinated references are flagged immediately — not discovered later.

Evidence hierarchy enforcement

Claims are tagged [RCT], [META], [COHORT], [PRECLINICAL], or [EXPERT]. A VERDICT requires RCT or META evidence — a cell line study cannot anchor a clinical recommendation.

Inference clearly labeled

Agent reasoning without a citation is prefixed INFERENCE: and excluded from verdict consideration. You always know what the literature supports versus what the model is inferring.

30 life science domains

Oncology, neuroscience, immunology, cardiology, infectious disease, pharmacology — each with a dedicated corpus built from domain-specific PubMed MeSH terms.

Full audit trail

Every turn, every citation, every DISAGREE flag is preserved. Export the complete transcript — agent reasoning and literature context included — for review or publication support.
